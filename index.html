This Master’s recital features original compositions and arrangements for a range of ensemble formats, from intimate chamber settings to full jazz big band.

Part one highlights small ensembles in duo, quartet, and sextet configurations performing original compositions. Part two unfolds with original jazz big band works influenced by jazz, blues, and Brazilian jazz traditions within contemporary practice.

This Master’s recital is presented with the support of the Black Box Theater, Salt Lake City. The artist gratefully acknowledges the UMass Jazz Department for its approval and academic support of this remote performance.

Part I · Small ensembles

Duo, quartet, and sextet pieces emphasizing melodic dialogue, rhythmic interplay, and close‑up improvisation.

Part II · Big band

Original large‑ensemble charts drawing on swing, blues inflection, and Brazilian rhythmic vocabulary for a modern big band sound.

Admission is free and open to the public.
